Overmind stores all of your chat history locally in your browser using `localStorage` — your conversations never leave your device, except when the content of a message is sent to the AI API to generate a response. There is no account, no cloud sync, and no server-side storage.

## Where Data Lives

Everything Overmind knows about you lives in your browser's `localStorage` under the following keys:

| Key | What it stores |
|---|---|
| `overmind_chats` | All chat conversations and their messages |
| `overmind_settings` | Your preferences (theme, tone, memory toggle, etc.) |
| `overmind_pins` | Any messages you've pinned across your chats |
| `overmind_daily_messages` | Your daily message count for the usage limit |

No account is required to use Overmind, and none of this data is uploaded to a server.

## Exporting Your Chats

You can download a full backup of all your conversations at any time.

1. Open **Settings → Privacy**.
2. Click **Export chats**.

Overmind downloads a file named `overmind-all-chats.json` containing every conversation in JSON format. You can also export the **current chat only** as a Markdown file using the keyboard shortcut <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>J</kbd> (or <kbd>Cmd</kbd>+<kbd>J</kbd> on Mac).

## Deleting All Chats

To permanently wipe every saved conversation from your browser:

1. Open **Settings → Privacy**.
2. Click **Clear all chats**.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

Overmind asks for confirmation before proceeding. Once you confirm, all chats and pinned messages are removed from `localStorage` and a new empty chat opens automatically.

<Warning>
  Clearing all chats is permanent and cannot be undone. Export your chats first if you want to keep a backup.
</Warning>

## Never Store Mode

If you'd rather Overmind never write your conversations to `localStorage` at all, toggle **Never store chat history** in **Settings → Privacy**. While this mode is active:

- No new messages are written to `localStorage`.
- Any chats you had previously saved are immediately cleared when you enable the toggle and save settings.
- Your conversation exists only in memory for the current browser tab session.
- Closing or refreshing the tab permanently discards everything.

Turn the toggle off (and save settings) at any time to resume normal storage.

<Note>
  Overmind's localStorage data is tied to your specific browser and device. Clearing your browser's site data, using private/incognito mode, or switching to a different browser will result in lost history that cannot be recovered.
</Note>
