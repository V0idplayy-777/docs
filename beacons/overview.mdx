Beacons is a one-line embed that brings Overmind's AI chat assistant to any website. Copy a single snippet from the Overmind app, paste it into your HTML, and your site instantly gains a fully functional AI chat experience. Beacons is currently in **beta**.

## How It Works

Once the snippet is on your page, a floating 💬 button appears in the bottom-right corner of every visitor's screen. Clicking the button smoothly opens a full-screen overlay panel — powered by an iframe — that loads the Overmind chat interface directly inside your page. Visitors can have a full conversation with Overmind without ever navigating away from your site. Clicking the button again collapses the panel just as smoothly.

## Use Cases

Beacons fits naturally into a wide range of web projects:

- **Personal portfolio or project site** — give visitors an AI assistant that can answer questions about your work
- **Landing pages** — let potential customers ask questions and get instant answers without waiting for a reply
- **Documentation or blogs** — provide interactive, conversational help alongside your written content
- **Any web experiment** — explore what AI-powered chat feels like embedded in your own projects

<Note>
  Beacons is currently in beta. The embed experience may change in future updates.
</Note>
