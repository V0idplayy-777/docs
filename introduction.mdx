Overmind is a browser-based AI chat assistant built for speed, clarity, and flexibility. Whether you're brainstorming ideas, writing code, or looking for quick answers, Overmind delivers streaming AI responses in a clean, distraction-free interface. Organize your conversations, extend functionality with plugins, and even embed Overmind directly on your own website — all without leaving the browser.
