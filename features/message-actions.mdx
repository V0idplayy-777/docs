Overmind gives you a set of per-message actions displayed below each message in the chat area. A row of small icon buttons sits beneath every message, letting you act on it without disrupting the rest of the conversation.

## Pinning Messages

Click the **pin icon** below any message to pin it. Pinned messages are visually distinguished from the rest of the chat:

- A green **📌 PINNED** badge appears above the message.
- The message gains a colored border and a subtle green glow.
- The background shifts to a tinted highlight color.

To unpin a message, click the pin icon again — the badge and styling are removed immediately.

<Tip>
Pin key pieces of information — like your project context or requirements — so Overmind always has them in view as your chat grows.
</Tip>

## Editing Your Messages

Click the **edit icon** below one of your own (user) messages to revise what you wrote. The message becomes editable, letting you correct a typo, add more detail, or rephrase your question. When you confirm the edit, Overmind re-sends the updated message and generates a fresh response.

<Note>
The edit action is only available on your own messages, not on Overmind's responses.
</Note>

## Regenerating Responses

Click the **regenerate icon** below any Overmind (bot) message to request a new response to the same prompt. Overmind will stream a replacement reply in place of the original. This is useful when a response misses the mark, is incomplete, or you simply want a different take on the same question.

<Note>
The regenerate action is only available on Overmind's responses, not on your own messages.
</Note>
