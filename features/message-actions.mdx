Overmind gives you a set of per-message actions accessible by hovering over any message in the chat area. A row of small icon buttons appears beneath the message, letting you act on it without disrupting the rest of the conversation.

## Pinning Messages

Hover over any message and click the **pin icon** to pin it. Pinned messages are visually distinguished from the rest of the chat:

- A green **📌 PINNED** badge appears above the message.
- The message gains a colored border and a subtle green glow.
- The background shifts to a tinted highlight color.

Pinned messages remain visible and are kept in context as your conversation grows. To unpin a message, hover over it and click the pin icon again — the badge and styling are removed immediately.

<Tip>
Pin key pieces of information — like your project context or requirements — so Overmind always has them in view as your chat grows.
</Tip>

## Copying Messages

Click the **copy icon** on any message to copy its full text content to your clipboard. This works for both your own messages and Overmind's responses, including formatted text and code blocks.

## Editing Your Messages

Hover over one of your own (user) messages and click the **edit icon** to revise what you wrote. The message becomes editable, letting you correct a typo, add more detail, or rephrase your question. When you confirm the edit, Overmind re-sends the updated message and generates a fresh response.

## Regenerating Responses

Click the **regenerate icon** on any Overmind (bot) message to request a new response to the same prompt. Overmind will stream a replacement reply in place of the original. This is useful when a response misses the mark, is incomplete, or you simply want a different take on the same question.
