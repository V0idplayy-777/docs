Overmind saves all your conversations locally in your browser and lets you manage them from the sidebar. You can create as many chats as you like, jump between them at any time, and keep things tidy with search, rename, and delete tools — all without leaving the app.

## Creating Chats

Click **+ New Chat** at the top of the sidebar to start a fresh conversation. Overmind creates a new empty session and switches to it immediately, showing the welcome screen and suggested prompts. Each chat is automatically given a title derived from the content of your first message, so you don't have to name anything yourself.

## Switching Chats

Click any entry in the sidebar chat list to switch to that conversation. The chat area updates instantly to show the selected session's messages. The **active chat** is highlighted in the list so you always know which session you're viewing.

## Searching Chats

Use the **search box** at the top of the sidebar to filter your chat list in real time. As you type, Overmind narrows the list to only show chats whose titles match your query. Clear the search field to restore the full list.

## Renaming & Deleting

Each chat item in the sidebar has a context menu you can open to manage that chat. From the menu you can:

- **Rename** — Give the chat a custom title that's easier to recognise later.
- **Delete** — Permanently remove the chat. Deleted chats are erased from local storage immediately and **cannot be recovered**, so confirm before deleting.

## Summarizing a Chat

In the top bar, click the **📝 Summarize Chat** button to ask Overmind to produce a compact summary of your current conversation. The summary streams into the chat area as a special **📝 Chat Summary** message, styled with a distinct border so it stands out from regular messages. This is useful when a conversation has grown long and you want a quick recap.

You can also trigger a summary with the keyboard shortcut **Ctrl+Shift+S** (or **Cmd+Shift+S** on macOS).

## Exporting Chats

To back up your conversations, open **Settings** and navigate to the **Privacy** tab. Click **Export chats** to download all your saved chats as a single `overmind-all-chats.json` file. You can use this file to archive your history or move it to another device. To export only the current chat as a Markdown file, use the keyboard shortcut **Ctrl+J** (or **Cmd+J** on macOS).
