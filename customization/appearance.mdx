Overmind's Appearance settings let you tailor the visual experience — from dark/light mode to typography and accent colors. Every option takes effect the moment you save, so you can experiment freely until the interface feels just right.

## Opening Settings

Click **Settings** in the sidebar, then select the **Appearance** tab.

---

## Theme

Choose between two theme modes to match your environment and preference.

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Dark** *(default)* | Deep black background (`#0a0a0a`) with a green accent (`#22c55e`). Easy on the eyes in low-light conditions. |
| **Light** | Clean white and light-gray palette. User messages switch to a darker green (`#16a34a`) for sufficient contrast against the white background. |

---

## Font

Pick the typeface that best suits your reading style. The selected font applies to the entire interface — messages, labels, and menus.

| Option | Typeface stack | Best for |
|--------|---------------|----------|
| **System** *(default)* | `system-ui`, `-apple-system`, `Segoe UI`, `Roboto`, sans-serif | Feels native to your operating system |
| **Serif** | `Georgia`, `Times New Roman`, `Times` | Long-form reading; classic editorial style |
| **Mono** | `SFMono-Regular`, `Consolas`, `Liberation Mono`, `Menlo` | Code-heavy conversations; developer workflows |

---

## Accent Color

The accent color drives the most visually prominent elements in Overmind: the send button, active tab highlights, pinned message badges, toggle switches, and more. Choose from the preset palette below.

| Color | Hex |
|-------|-----|
| **Green** *(default)* | `#22c55e` |
| **Blue** | `#3b82f6` |
| **Purple** | `#a855f7` |
| **Red** | `#ef4444` |
| **Orange** | `#f59e0b` |
| **Pink** | `#ec4899` |
| **Cyan** | `#06b6d4` |
| **White** | `#ffffff` |

<Note>
  The accent color also themes the emoji avatar displayed in the top bar, so the whole interface shifts cohesively when you change it.
</Note>

---

## Emoji Style

The **Emoji usage** selector controls how often emojis appear inside Overmind's responses.

| Option | Behavior |
|--------|----------|
| **None** | Overmind never uses emojis in replies. |
| **Occasional** *(default)* | Emojis appear when they fit naturally in context. |
| **Frequent** | Overmind uses emojis liberally to add warmth and personality. |

---

## Saving Your Changes

When you are done adjusting, click **Save** at the bottom of the Settings modal. Your preferences are stored locally in your browser and restored automatically the next time you open Overmind.
