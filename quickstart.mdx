This page walks you through everything you need to go from zero to your first productive Overmind session. By the end, you'll know how to send messages, manage your chats, and tailor the experience to suit your style.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Overmind">
    Navigate to the Overmind app in your browser. You'll see the **sidebar** on the left — your full list of saved chats — and the **main chat area** taking up the rest of the screen. The welcome message greets you with: *"I am Overmind. What do you seek?"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your first message">
    Click the text area at the bottom of the screen, type your question or request, and press **Enter** or click the **↑** send button. Overmind streams the response back to you in real time, with a blinking cursor while it's thinking — no waiting for a full response to load.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a new chat">
    Click **+ New Chat** in the top-left corner of the sidebar to open a fresh conversation. Each chat is saved automatically the moment you start it and appears in your chat list so you can return to it at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your experience">
    Click **Settings** in the sidebar to open the settings panel. From there you can change the theme (dark or light), accent color, font, conversation tone, and more. Click **Save** when you're happy with your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Overmind currently allows up to **10 messages per day** per browser session. Your count resets each day.
</Note>
